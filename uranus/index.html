Uranus Transits and Returns

Uranus takes approximately 84 years to complete its orbit, creating significant life cycles:

Uranus Opposition (Age 38-42)

The midlife crisis transit. Sudden career changes, relationship upheavals, or radical lifestyle shifts.

Uranus Square (Age 19-22 & 63-66)

Breaking away from family patterns, establishing independence, or retirement revolutions.

Uranus Return (Age 84)

Complete life cycle completion. Wisdom sharing, legacy creation, or radical elderhood.
