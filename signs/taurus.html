2. Taurus Personality Traits

Taurus represents stability in motion. Once committed, Taurus builds patiently and steadily, turning ideas into tangible reality.

Core Taurus Energy: Taurus embodies the principle of “I HAVE.” It teaches mastery of the material world through patience, consistency, and appreciation of beauty.

3. Symbolism & Mythology

The Bull of Heaven

In mythology, Taurus is linked to Zeus transforming into a white bull to carry Europa across the sea — symbolizing desire, abundance, and divine attraction.
