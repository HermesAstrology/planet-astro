3. The Three Qualities (Modalities)

Each zodiac sign has one of three qualities that describe how they approach life and express their energy:

Cardinal Signs

Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn

Initiators who start new projects and seasons. They're action-oriented, ambitious, and love to lead.

Fixed Signs

Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius

Sustainers who maintain and deepen what's been started. They're determined, reliable, and resistant to change.

Mutable Signs

Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces

Adaptors who change and adjust. They're flexible, versatile, and help with transitions between seasons.

Seasonal Connection: Cardinal signs begin each season (Aries = Spring, Cancer = Summer, Libra = Fall, Capricorn = Winter). Fixed signs represent the height of each season. Mutable signs represent the transition between seasons.
