Shadows & Growth Edges

Primary Challenge: Avoiding the trap of believing "I am what I own." The spiritual task is to cultivate an unshakable sense of inner worth that exists independently of your resources, while still enjoying the material world.

Growth comes from learning flexibility within your stability, practicing generosity to avoid hoarding tendencies, and investing in experiences and relationships with the same care you invest in material assets.
