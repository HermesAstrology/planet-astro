The Planet of Dreams and Illusions

Neptune represents spirituality, dreams, illusions, creativity, and transcendence. It shows where you experience mysticism, compassion, and connection to the collective unconscious.

The Mystical and The Mystifying

Neptune dissolves boundaries, creating both spiritual transcendence and confusing illusions. It is the planet of both divine inspiration and deceptive fog.
