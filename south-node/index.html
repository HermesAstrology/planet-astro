Understanding the South Node

Core Meanings

  • Past Lives: Karmic patterns & history
  • Comfort Zone: Familiar territory & habits
  • Mastery: Skills already developed
  • Release: What to let go of
  • Debts: Karmic obligations & lessons

What the South Node Shows

  • Past life professions & roles
  • Automatic behaviors & instincts
  • Areas of over-development
  • Karmic relationships patterns
  • Soul's accumulated wisdom

Astrological Details

  • Type: Lunar Node (mathematical point)
  • Direction: Always retrograde
  • Cycle: 18.6 years (full cycle)
  • Name: Also called Ketu
  • Opposite: Always opposite North Node

Karmic Relationships Note: When someone's planet conjuncts your South Node, it often indicates a past life connection. These relationships can feel instantly familiar but may also bring karmic patterns that need healing and release.
