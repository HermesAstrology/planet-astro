Each Moon sign has specific emotional requirements for feeling secure and content:

Need: Stability, practical security, tangible comforts

Air Moons (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)

Need: Mental stimulation, social connection, freedom to communicate

Fire Moons (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
